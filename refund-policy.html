Refund Policy

At Listed Stays, we strive to provide you with an excellent experience when booking hotels, resorts, and accommodations through our platform. This Refund Policy explains how we handle refund requests and outlines the circumstances under which refunds may be granted. By using our website and services, you agree to the terms of this Refund Policy.

1. General Refund Information

Refunds for bookings made through Listed Stays are subject to the terms and conditions of the specific accommodation provider (hotel, resort, etc.) with whom the booking was made. Each hotel or resort may have its own cancellation and refund policy, which is made available to you at the time of booking. It is your responsibility to review these terms before finalizing your reservation.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refund eligibility depends on the cancellation policy of the accommodation provider and the type of booking made. In general, refunds may be granted under the following circumstances:

A. Cancellation of Booking

  • Non-Refundable Bookings: If you have selected a non-refundable rate, your booking is not eligible for a refund if you cancel it.
  • Refundable Bookings: For refundable bookings, you are eligible for a refund if you cancel the reservation within the time frame outlined in the accommodation provider’s cancellation policy. The cancellation window varies by hotel and rate type.
  • Late Cancellations: If you cancel after the specified cancellation window, you may be subject to a cancellation fee or forfeit the full amount of the booking.

B. Overcharges or Incorrect Charges

If you believe you have been overcharged or incorrectly billed, please contact our support team at info@listedstays.com as soon as possible. We will review the charges and, if applicable, process a refund for the overcharge.

C. Booking Errors

In the event of an error on our part (e.g., technical error causing double booking, incorrect charges), we will work with the accommodation provider to process a refund.

3. Refund Process

If you meet the eligibility criteria for a refund, please follow these steps:

  • Step 1: Contact the accommodation provider directly to confirm your refund eligibility. You can find their contact information in your booking confirmation email.
  • Step 2: If necessary, reach out to Listed Stays customer service at info@listedstays.com for assistance.
  • Step 3: We will verify the refund request and, if approved, work with the accommodation provider to process the refund according to their policy.

Refunds will typically be issued to the original method of payment, unless otherwise agreed upon.

4. Time Frame for Refunds

The time frame for processing refunds depends on the accommodation provider’s policy and the payment method used. Once a refund is approved, you can generally expect to receive the refund within 7-14 business days. However, some refunds may take longer depending on your payment provider and processing times.

5. Refunds for Special Circumstances

  • Force Majeure (Unforeseen Events): In cases where your booking is impacted by extraordinary events such as natural disasters, pandemics, or other emergencies beyond our control, we will work with the accommodation provider to issue a refund or offer alternative accommodations. These situations may require additional documentation.
  • Health or Medical Emergencies: If you are unable to travel due to a medical emergency, please contact us with supporting documentation. Refunds are granted at the discretion of the accommodation provider, but we will do our best to assist you in this case.

6. Refunds for Unused Nights

If you check out early or shorten your stay, you may not be entitled to a refund for unused nights. However, in certain cases, accommodations may offer a partial refund or credit for future bookings. Refunds for unused nights are subject to the accommodation provider’s discretion.

7. Changes to Bookings

If you need to make changes to your booking (e.g., changing dates, upgrading rooms), these changes are also subject to the accommodation provider’s policies. Depending on the change, a refund or additional charges may apply.

8. Non-Refundable Fees

Certain fees and charges may not be refundable, including but not limited to:

  • Resort fees
  • Booking service fees
  • Cleaning or processing fees
  • Special event or promotional rates that are explicitly non-refundable

9. Refunds for Fraudulent Transactions

If we determine that a transaction was fraudulent, we will work with the relevant authorities and take necessary action to prevent further fraudulent activity. Refunds for fraudulent transactions will not be processed.

10. Contact Us for Refund Requests

If you have any questions or need assistance with a refund request, please contact our customer support team at info@listedstays.com. Provide the following details to help us process your request faster:

  • Booking reference number
  • Full name
  • Reason for refund request
  • Relevant documentation (if applicable)

11. Final Terms

  • Refund Decisions: All refund requests are subject to approval by the accommodation provider, and Listed Stays will not be held responsible for any decision made by the accommodation provider.
  • Changes to the Refund Policy: Listed Stays reserves the right to modify or update this Refund Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Effective Date" will be updated accordingly.

By making a booking through Listed Stays, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to the terms of this Refund Policy.
