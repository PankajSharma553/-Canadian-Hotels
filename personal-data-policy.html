Personal Data Policy

At Listed Stays, we value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal data. This Personal Data Policy describes how we collect, store, use, and protect your personal information when you interact with our website or use our services. By using our website, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Overview of Personal Data

Personal data refers to any information that can directly or indirectly identify you, such as your name, email address, phone number, and other identifiable information. We are committed to processing your personal data in compliance with applicable data protection laws and regulations.

2. Types of Personal Data We Collect

We collect the following types of personal data when you interact with our website or services:

A. Personal Identification Information

  • Name (First and Last)
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Mailing Address
  • Billing Information (such as credit card details)
  • Account Information (when you create an account on our website)

B. Non-Personal Identification Information

  • IP Address
  • Device Information (mobile, desktop, etc.)
  • Browser Type
  • Operating System
  • Pages Visited
  • Time and Date of Visits
  • Cookies and Tracking Data

3. How We Collect Your Personal Data

We collect your personal data through the following methods:

A. Direct Collection

We may collect personal data directly from you when you:

  • Register for an account on our website.
  • Make a reservation or booking.
  • Subscribe to our newsletter.
  • Contact us for customer support or inquiries.

B. Automated Collection

We collect non-personal data automatically as you interact with our website. This includes:

  • Tracking your browsing behavior and website usage through cookies.
  • Collecting your device and browser information to improve your experience. .

C. Third-Party Collection

We may also receive personal data from third-party services, such as:

  • Payment processors (when making a booking).
  • Social media platforms (if you use these platforms to sign in or interact with us).

4. Purposes for Which We Use Your Personal Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Our Services: To process your bookings, respond to inquiries, and facilitate your interaction with hotels and resorts.
  • To Improve User Experience: To personalize and enhance your browsing experience on our website.
  • To Communicate with You: To send transactional emails (e.g., booking confirmations) and promotional communications (if you have opted in).
  • For Analytics and Research: To analyze website traffic and performance to optimize user experience.
  • For Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations, enforce our Terms and Conditions, or address legal disputes.

5. Legal Basis for Processing Your Personal Data

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Performance of a Contract: To fulfill our obligations related to your bookings, account, and interactions with our website.
  • Consent: When you provide your consent (e.g., subscribing to our newsletter).
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our website, enhance your user experience, and offer relevant products and services.
  • Legal Obligations: To comply with applicable laws, respond to legal requests, and protect our rights and interests.

6. How We Protect Your Personal Data

We implement a variety of security measures to ensure the protection of your personal data:

  • Encryption: We use SSL encryption for sensitive data, such as payment information.
  • Firewalls and Secure Servers: We employ firewalls and secure servers to protect against unauthorized access.
  • Access Control: Access to your personal data is restricted to authorized personnel who need the information to perform their job duties.

While we take reasonable measures to protect your personal data, no data transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

7. Sharing Your Personal Data

We may share your personal data with third parties in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share your data with third-party service providers who help us process bookings, payment transactions, or provide customer support. These providers are obligated to safeguard your data and use it only for purposes related to our services.
  • Legal Compliance: We may share your data when required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or part of our business, your personal data may be transferred to the new owner.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect non-personal data and improve your browsing experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and analyze website traffic. You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling them may impact your user experience.

For more information, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

9. Your Rights Over Your Personal Data

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete personal data we have about you.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request that we delete your personal data, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: You can request that we restrict the processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request a copy of your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: You can object to the processing of your personal data, especially in relation to direct marketing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@listedstays.com.

10. Data Retention

We will retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. Once the data is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

11. Children’s Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that data.

12. Changes to This Personal Data Policy

We may update this Personal Data Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Effective Date" will be updated. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your personal data.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Personal Data Policy or if you would like to exercise your data protection rights, please contact us at:

Email: info@listedstays.com
